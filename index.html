Decay

CycleGAN predicts what inorganic objects would look like when they decay

A deep neural network was trained to predict the "decayed" appearance of an arbitrary input image.A dataset that consists of several hundred images of fresh/rotten apples, oranges, and bananas were used to train the neural network. pix2pix, CycleGAN, and DiscoGAN were considered as our neural network model, and CycleGAN was found to perform the best.

Receipt Grand Total Extractor

A simple online receipt grand total extractor

This is a simple online receipt grand total extractor. It utilizes pytesseract as an OCR(Optical Character Recognition) engine, along with openCV computer vision library to enhance the recognization ability. This app is written in python and is deployed with Google Cloud Run.
