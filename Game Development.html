1006Games

November 2019 - Present

YoungWoong is a co-founder of a video game company 1006Games with his teammates. He is currently working as a Game developer and Lead Design Manager in 1006Games, an indie game company that consists of three developers.

A project that was accomplished out of pure curiosity

YoungWoong Cho, as an enthusiastic gamer, always had a dream of making his own games; a proposition of Sebeom Lee, co-founder and lead game developer of 1006Games, was a great opportunity for him to take a step closer to his yet-to-be-realized dream.

Since the foundation of 1006Games in November 2019, YoungWoong had actively participated in developing video games.

SPLUX

December 2019 - May 2020

SPLUX is the first game released by 1006Games. SPLUX is a multiplayer tetris game in which each player can not only eliminate lines to score but also invade other players' zones to sabotage.
SPLUX is available on Google Play and CrazyGames.
